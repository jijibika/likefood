Reservations

Although reservations are not required, we highly encourage them especially on weekends and for large parties. Reservations can be booked through OpenTable and by calling us during business hours.

We offer reservations for both INDOOR (standard or high-top) and OUTDOOR (patio) seating. Please confirm your choice at the time of reserving your table. All seating options are listed unless they are no longer available. Our bar top is full service and available on a first come, first serve basis. Please note we do our absolute best to honor requests. However, we cannot guarantee them.

We have a 15 minute grace period. Please call us if you are running later than 15 minutes after your reservation time to ensure we save your space.

Parties larger than 8 will not be available to be booked online, please call us for availability or email us at events@moraitalian.com

We do require a credit card to secure reservations for parties of 8+. All credit card information will be processed securely. No-shows or cancellations less than 2 days in advance will be subject to a charge of $150.

Parties of 6 or more are subject to a 20% automatic gratuity. We can only accept 4 forms of payment per party.
